Fruits And Vegetable -TOMATOES



TOMATOES



Varieties include:

Select:

Brightly coloured tomatoes free from blemishes.

Store:

At room temperature away from direct sunlight to allow for natural ripening. To avoid over- ripening, store in refrigerator crisper.

Serving suggestion:

Can be used in sandwiches, salads, soups, casseroles, vegetable side dishes, sauces and dressings. Roma tomatoes are great for cooking!

Why eat tomatoes?



‘The Australian Guide to Healthy Eating, Commonwealth Copyright 1998 is reproduced with permission of the Australian Government Department of Health and Ageing, 2010’. They are an excellent source of antioxidant vitamins C, A and lycopene. They are a tasty and colourful addition to most meals!
